Family Support for Drug Addiction Recovery

Why Family Support Matters

Addiction affects not only the individual but also their family. Our family support sessions are designed to provide education, guidance, and a safe space for families to understand and cope with the challenges of addiction.

Online Family Counseling Sessions

We offer weekly counseling sessions, 7 days a week, to support families. Each session includes:

  • Real-time video sessions with experienced counselors
  • Understanding addiction and its impact on families
  • Effective communication strategies
  • Sharing experiences and learning from others
